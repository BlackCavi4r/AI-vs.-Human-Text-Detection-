Nowadays, many people think that technology has become a necessary part of school life. In my opinion, the most important thing is not only having new tools, but knowing how to use them in a specific way. Furthermore, students do not all learn in the same environment. Some students like doing work on paper, while others prefer a computer because it helps them keep order. This essay explains the advantages and disadvantages of using digital tools for studying. Consequently, the best solution depends on the student's habits, the class requirements, and the amount of time available during the week.
